MESHADOW

Let your shadow point to the light way.

Objective

The MeShadow is media for student to unidentify and shows many problem in university.

Concept

1. Humans are often afraid of expressing in a good way.


2. But they will dare in the dark side.


3. So, they use the shadow as a dark and the way as problem.


4. Shadow will point many ways, and it means that the shadow reveal all the problems to be seen.


5. If the way is cleaned, it will be usable.


6. So the cleaned way is the solved problem.


Concept

1. The university is a place for knowledge and development for students.


2. Students are the product of university to be a future of the nation.


3. If students have a good quality life, they will be a good person.


4. So the problem occurred on university. It will affect the students as well.


5. Of course, many students do not dare show problems.


6. It is wrong. MeShadow was developed to solve this problem.
